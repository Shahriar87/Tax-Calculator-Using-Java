Bio

Pink Floyd, an English rock band formed in London, UK back in 1965. They are recognised for their unique psychedelic genre of music followed by philosophical lyrics, sonic experimentation, extended compositions & elaborate live shows.

Pink Floyd were founded by students Syd Barrett on guitar and lead vocals, Nick Mason on drums, Roger Waters on bass and vocals, and Richard Wright on keyboards and vocals. They gained popularity performing in London's underground music scene during the late 1960s, and under Barrett's leadership released two charting singles and a successful debut album, The Piper at the Gates of Dawn (1967). Guitarist and vocalist David Gilmour joined in December 1967.
